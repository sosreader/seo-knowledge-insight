Thursday, June 20, 2024

We're coming back to Thailand with [Search Central Live](/search/events/search-central-live)! As mentioned in our blog post about [our APAC plans for SCL](/search/blog/2024/05/search-central-live-jakarta-apac-2024), we now have a date and a site where you can sign up for a chance to secure your spot at Search Central Live Bangkok 2024.

![Search Central Live bangkok 2024](/static/search/blog/images/search-central-live-apac/search-central-live-bangkok-2024-logo.png)

After 5 years of hiatus, on **August 9, 2024**, we're bringing back our flagship Search event for site owners to Thailand. As with other events, we chose topics that are relevant to the local audience and that we think will be the most useful, including:

* How Search works
* How to think about content quality and updates
* Analyzing traffic drops with Search Console
* Google Trends for SEOs

The talks will be in Thai and English, with interpretation service for those who don't speak one of the languages. Before you pack your bags for a transcontinental flight, the talks and activities are tailored for a local audience and thus **we're only accepting folks living in Thailand and neighboring countries for the event**. We also recommend that attendees have **a basic command of Thai** to enjoy the event to its fullest.

If you fit that profile and you're interested in learning from the Search team, register your interest before July 22. If your spot is confirmed, we will let you know by July 29. If you are reading this blog, use a registration code: SearchCentral. We can’t guarantee your seat but it will help us in the selection process.

[Register](https://rsvp.withgoogle.com/events/search-central-live-bangkok-2024/home)

See you soon, and please bring lots of durian sticky rice for Gary and ya dom for everyone else! (that was a joke; please don't bring durian to the venue.)
