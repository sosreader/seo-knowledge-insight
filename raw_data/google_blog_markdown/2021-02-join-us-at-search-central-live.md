## Introducing Search Central Live

Since our move under the new [Google Search Central](/search) umbrella, we've been
working on creating a new series of virtual events to explore different ways to gather and
interact online.

[Search
Central Live](https://eventsonair.withgoogle.com/events/search-central-live) is an experiment to try out a livestream format rather than a pre-recorded set
of talks. We encourage you to join the event live on YouTube and interact with the speakers
through the chat in real time. We hope to create an interesting dialogue between the community and
the speakers this way.

We are also piloting how we can host community speakers as part of the event, in order to foster
the dialog between Google Search and the broader community of SEOs, developers, and website owners.

## What to expect from Search Central Live?

This event is a pilot to see how we can use virtual live events to build an interactive experience
as well as foster a dialogue with the community as part of our virtual events. It will be a livestream
that you can access through the [event
website](https://eventsonair.withgoogle.com/events/search-central-live). After the event, the content will be available on-demand on our YouTube channel in
English with captions. The event is available without payment, the registration is open to everyone, and there is no
limit to the number of participants. The first event on February 24, 2021 JST is going to be centered
around Japan, and future events will accommodate other timezones.

This Search Central Live event will consist of three sessions:

1. **A fireside chat** with [Cherry Prommawin](https://www.linkedin.com/in/cherry-prom/), [Danny Sullivan](https://mastodon.social/@searchliaison),
   [Ashley Berman Hale](https://www.unitedsearch.org/),
   and [Martin Splitt](https://50linesofco.de)
2. A live **technical SEO case study** with [Kristina
   Azarenko](https://marketingsyrup.com/) and [Martin Splitt](https://50linesofco.de)
3. A live session of **Search Off The Record** with [Gary Illyes](https://garyillyes.com/+), [John Mueller](https://johnmu.com/), and
   [Martin Splitt](https://50linesofco.de)

You can find the agenda on the [event website](https://eventsonair.withgoogle.com/events/search-central-live) as well.
