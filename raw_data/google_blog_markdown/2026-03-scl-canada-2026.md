# Search Central Live is coming to Canada!

Wednesday, March 11, 2026

Our event series, Search Central Live, is coming to Toronto, Canada on April 21, 2026 for the first time!
The event series has been a great way for communities to connect with each other and Googlers to learn
more about the web ecosystem and how Google Search works and exchange ideas.

![Search Central Live Canada 2026 banner](/static/search/blog/images/scl-canada-2026.jpg)

The event will have presenters from the Google Search Relations and other Google teams. The content will be presented in English. Topics include:

* Better understanding crawling, indexing, and AI in Search
* Recent updates in Google Search
* Best practices for ecommerce structured data
* Practical tips for Search Console and Google Trends
* And much more!

Besides presentations and a Q&A session, there will be opportunities to meet Googlers and other attendees.

This event is aimed at website owners, publishers, professionals working with news organizations,
digital marketers, web developers, SEO professionals, or folks otherwise interested in websites in Search.
So if you're interested in these topics, work with websites and want to join us, apply for a seat to join us there!

The event won't be recorded or live streamed, that's why we call it live!

## Register now

[Apply for an invitation by April 14, 2026](https://rsvp.withgoogle.com/events/search-central-live-canada-2026)

Unfortunately, we can't accommodate everyone. Sign up until April 14th.
We'll send invitations by April 16th to accepted attendees, you'll need that email to enter the event.
The event will take place on April 21, between 9:00 and 14:00 in Toronto (exact address will follow with the invitation).

Check more details on our [event website](https://rsvp.withgoogle.com/events/search-central-live-canada-2026).
