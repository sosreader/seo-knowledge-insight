Monday, August 03, 2020

As the Search Relations team at Google, we're here to help site owners be successful with their
websites in Google Search. We write on this blog, create and maintain the
[Google Search documentation](/search), produce videos
on our [YouTube channel](https://www.youtube.com/channel/UCWf2ZlNsCGDS89VBF_awNvA?sub_confirmation=1)
- and now we're making a [Search Off The
Record podcast](https://search-off-the-record.libsyn.com/), too!

In the podcast, [John](https://twitter.com/johnmu),
[Gary Illyes](https://garyillyes.com/+) and [Martin](https://50linesofco.de)
from our team are talking about a variety of topics and give a glimpse behind the scenes of
Google Search.

In this podcast, we discuss things that are on our minds, like:

* updates in Google Search
* background on recent announcements
* news from the SEO and web developer communities
* stories from the past
* things we're working on
* food and drinks
* random things from the top of our heads
* puns, jokes and more
