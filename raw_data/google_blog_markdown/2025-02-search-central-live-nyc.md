# Search Central Live is going to New York City

Thursday, February 13, 2025

We're excited to announce that Search Central Live is coming to New York City for the first time on March 20, 2025.
The Google Search team has organized events in the city several times, but this time we're bringing our main Search event to the city that never sleeps.
Our goal is to create an environment where we can discuss how our systems work and hear feedback from you.

![Search Central Live New York City logo](/static/search/blog/images/scl-nyc-2025.png)

We'll have a broad range of speakers from different Google teams, discussing the following topics:

* What's top of mind for the Google Search team
* How Search works & SEO myths
* What's new in Search Console and how to make it useful
* Search partnerships, Google News, and more!

If these topics sound interesting to you, don't miss your chance to participate.
You'll also have time to chat with the Google Search and News teams, and meet new people in real life.
If you're in or around NYC on March 20, 2025, don't miss this opportunity!

[Learn more & apply for an invitation](https://rsvp.withgoogle.com/events/search-central-live-nyc-2025)

Unfortunately, we can't accommodate everyone.
Applications are open until February 26, and we'll send invitations on March 3, 2025 to accepted attendees (you'll need that email to enter the event).

We're looking forward to meeting you in person and chatting about Google Search and News.
