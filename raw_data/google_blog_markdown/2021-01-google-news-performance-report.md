# Introducing Google News performance report

- **來源類型**: article
- **來源集合**: google-search-central
---
Tuesday, January 12, 2021

Today we are launching Google News performance reporting to help news publishers better understand user behavior on Google News on our [Android](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.google.android.apps.magazines) and [iOS](https://apps.apple.com/app/google-news/id459182288) apps, as well as on [news.google.com](https://news.google.com).

Publishers can access their performance report through [Search Console](https://search.google.com/search-console/about) by clicking **Google News** in the left navigation's performance section. The report will include metrics like impressions, clicks, and click-through rate (CTR) that will help publishers answer questions like:

* How many times have my articles appeared to users in Google News?
* Which articles performed well in Google News?
* How does user behavior vary by country?

![Google News performance report in Search Console](/static/search/blog/images/google-news-report-search-console.png)

This report will appear for any site that appears in Google News. These insights are in addition to Search Console reports about [Google Search](https://www.google.com/), [Discover](/search/blog/2019/04/search-console-reporting-for-your-sites), and the News tab on Search. Learn more about [news content appearing in Google News](https://support.google.com/news/publisher-center/answer/9607025).

For other questions or feedback on the new performance report, visit our [Search Central help community](https://support.google.com/webmasters/go/community).
