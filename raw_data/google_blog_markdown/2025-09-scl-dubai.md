Tuesday, September 23, 2025

For the first time, Search Central Live is coming to Dubai on October 21, 2025!
After events in Europe, Latin America, and Asia, we're continuing our mission to help
you enhance your site's performance in Google Search, live in Dubai, UAE.

![Search Central Live Dubai 2025](/static/search/blog/images/search-central-live-emea/search-central-live-dubai-2025.png "Search Central Live Dubai 2025")

The event will have presenters from the Google Search Relations and Search Console teams.
The content will be presented in English. Topics include:

* Better understanding crawling, indexing, and AI
* Recent updates in Google Search & Discover
* Best practices to succeed in the modern Google Search
* Practical tips for Search Console and Google Trends

If these topics sound interesting to you, if you're located in the broader region, and if you're
a website owner, publisher, working with news organizations, a digital marketer, web developer,
SEO professionals, or otherwise interested in websites in Search, register now for a chance to join us there.
Apart from the presentations, you'll have lots of time to chat with the Google Search team, and
meet new people in real life. The event won't be recorded or live streamed, that's why we call it live!

## Apply by October 5

[Apply for an invitation](https://rsvp.withgoogle.com/events/search-central-live-dubai-2025)

Unfortunately, we can't accommodate everyone. Please sign up by Sunday, October 5.
We'll send invitations by October 10 to accepted attendees, you'll need that email to enter the event.
The event will take place on October 21, between 9:15 and 17:30, at Bluewaters Island, Dubai, UAE
(exact address will follow with the invitation).
Check for more details
[on
our event website](https://rsvp.withgoogle.com/events/search-central-live-dubai-2025).

## See you there!

We're looking forward to meeting in person and chatting about Google Search and more!
