Thursday, March 12, 2020

Last year we organized
[Webmaster Conference](/search/blog/2019/06/webmaster-conference)
events in over 15 countries. The spirit of WMConf is reflected in that number: we want to reach
regions that otherwise don't get much search conference love. Recently we have been monitoring
the coronavirus (COVID-19) situation and its impact on planning this year's events.

To wit, with the growing concern around coronavirus and in line with the travel guidelines
published by [WHO](https://www.who.int/emergencies/diseases/novel-coronavirus-2019),
the [CDC](https://www.cdc.gov/coronavirus/2019-ncov/index), and other organizations,
we are postponing all Webmaster Conference events globally. While we're hoping to organize
events later this year, we're also exploring other ways to reach targeted audiences.

We're very sorry to delay the opportunity to connect in person, but we feel strongly that the
safety and health of all attendees is the priority at this time. If you want to get notified
about future events in your region, you can sign up to receive updates on the
[Webmaster Conference site](/search/events).
If you have questions or comments, catch us on
[Twitter](https://twitter.com/googlesearchc)!
