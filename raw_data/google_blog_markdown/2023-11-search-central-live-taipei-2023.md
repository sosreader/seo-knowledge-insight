We are ecstatic to announce that for the first time ever, Search Central Live is coming to Taipei! Part of the [Search Central Live events series](/search/events/search-central-live), this is the first in-person event we are organizing in Taiwan, and we're coming prepared with a busy agenda and ready to answer (most) of your questions.

If you speak Mandarin fluently and are interested, come join us at **Search Central Live Taipei 2023** on December 6!
