Wednesday, April 21, 2021

We published episode 4 of our webtoon series in Korean. Here's a summary of the episode in English:

When Choco the father confidently believes that uploading a whole batch of high quality content
alone will ensure high traffic to his site, Cherry and Eco tell Choco about the snippets in search
results pages and the importance of meta description tag. They also educate the father what a
sitemap does especially for a new site and the usefulness of Search Console. Choco is now ready to
register his site on Search Console.
