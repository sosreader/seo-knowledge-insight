# Search Central Live Hong Kong 2025: Event in Chinese focusing on international ecommerce

Monday, September 8, 2025

As part of our broader APAC plan, which includes new Deep Dive events and two local language
editions, we are happy to announce the details for our Chinese language event:
**Search Central Live Hong Kong!**

On October 31, 2025, we will bring Google speakers to you in Hong Kong to talk about Search and
explore together the unique challenges and opportunities of optimizing your online stores for the
international market.

![Search Central Live Hong Kong 2025](/static/search/blog/images/search-central-live-apac/search-central-live-hk-2025-logo.png)

## What to Expect?

Search Central Live Hong Kong will be mainly conducted in Chinese. It features a mix of
presentations, Q&A, and networking, designed to provide a comprehensive learning experience.

The event will take place on October 31, 2025. The presentations will cover a wide range of SEO
topics, but with additional focus on ecommerce SEO, internationalization, and localization. If you
are fluent in Chinese and are interested, register now to express interest in attending; we'll
confirm your spot by October 20, 2025.
