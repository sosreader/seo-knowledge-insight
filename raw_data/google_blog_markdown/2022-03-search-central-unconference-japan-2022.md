Thursday, March 24, 2022

It's been a while since we published this blog post. Some of the information may be outdated (for example, some images may be missing, and some links may not work anymore). For information on upcoming events, check out our new [Search Central Unconference page](/search/events/search-central-live).

The [Search Central Virtual Unconference Japan 2022](https://rsvp.withgoogle.com/events/search-central-virtual-unconference-japan-2022) is now open for registration. The sessions will be conducted in Japanese. If you are interested, be sure to register by March 30! Come meet the community, exchange ideas, and have fun together.

This is on top of the [global Search Central Unconference 2022](https://rsvp.withgoogle.com/events/search-central-virtual-unconference-2022) which is scheduled for April 27.
