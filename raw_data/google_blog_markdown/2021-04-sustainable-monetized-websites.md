Monday, April 12, 2021

The new series [Sustainable Monetized Websites](https://www.youtube.com/playlist?list=PLKoqnv2vTMUMtJE13ilkN7sNaUgfWmFzG)
is now available on the [Search Central](https://www.youtube.com/channel/UCWf2ZlNsCGDS89VBF_awNvA?sub_confirmation=1)
and the [Google AdSense](https://www.youtube.com/channel/UCWZeRDL6a3QTAd1FSz3Tmjg) YouTube channels. This series focuses on the best practices
for people who create or manage content online and monetize it, acknowledging how challenging this can be and assisting them with resources and focus
areas... all in one place!

Even though you might think that the organic and monetized worlds are very different, the truth is, no matter who you are,
everything can be distilled into this mantra—*focus on your users: create great content*.

Throughout the series, Aurora and her two alter-ego co-hosts, Organica (Search Champion) and Monetized (Publishing Guru), share policies, guidelines,
and best practices around [traffic fluctuation](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=D1hg28sDhHY),
[user-generated content](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=GKq9C0GlqfQ),
[duplicate](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=7rFcGcZg9O4) and
[thin](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ZLTz3KRsy4k) content, among other topics.

Future episodes will include useful tools, such as [Search Console](https://search.google.com/search-console),
the [Publisher Policy center](https://support.google.com/adsense/answer/10008391) and
[Site Kit](https://sitekit.withgoogle.com/)—which can be powerful when used together to improve your online presence.
Make sure to subscribe to the [Search Central channel](https://www.youtube.com/channel/UCWf2ZlNsCGDS89VBF_awNvA?sub_confirmation=1) to be
the first to know when new videos are published.

In a really challenging year, we've had to be creative about how to share these best practices while staying safe! By turning our homes into film sets,
we're able to keep creating content to help you get the most out of the web.

We hope you enjoy this series as much as we’ve enjoyed making it!
