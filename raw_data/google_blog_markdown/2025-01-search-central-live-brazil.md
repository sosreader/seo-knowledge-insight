# Search Central Live is returning to Brazil in 2025

Wednesday, January 15, 2025

[Leia em Português](/search/blog/2025/01/search-central-live-brazil?hl=pt-br)

We're excited to announce that Search Central Live is returning to São Paulo in 2025. Following our
successful events in 2023 and 2024, we're continuing our mission to help Brazilian businesses enhance
their site's performance in Google Search. And this year we're visiting Recife for the first time.

![Search Central Live Brazil logo](/static/search/blog/images/search-central-live-brazil.png)

We'd like to invite SEOs, content creators, website owners, developers, journalists, and anyone interested
in Google Search to apply for an invite on the event websites. We have two events you can apply for:

* São Paulo: February 18, 2025, at 09:00
* Recife: February 20, 2025, at 14:00

[Apply for São Paulo](https://rsvp.withgoogle.com/events/search-central-live-brasil-sao-2025)

[Apply for Recife](https://rsvp.withgoogle.com/events/search-central-live-recife)

We'll have a broad range of speakers, including [Bruno Possas](https://www.linkedin.com/in/bruno-possas-40551089/),
VP Engineering at Google, who leads Google Search teams from our own Belo Horizonte office. We'll also
have members from the Search Console and Google Trends teams to talk about how you can best use these
products. Some presentations will be delivered in Portuguese and some in English, for those we'll have
live translation.

The speakers will discuss the following:

* How Google Search works and why we do updates?
* How Search uses AI and what can you do with it?
* What have the Search Console and Trends teams been working on?
* How can you measure SEO efforts using Search Console and Google Analytics?
* How is Google Brazil partnering with local companies in Search and News?

If these topics sound interesting to you, don't miss your chance to participate. You'll also have time
to chat with folks from Google Search and News teams, and meet new people in real life. The event won't
be recorded or live streamed, that's why we call it live! Unfortunately we can't accommodate everyone,
so don't miss this opportunity, apply for an invite to
[the São Paulo](https://rsvp.withgoogle.com/events/search-central-live-brasil-sao-2025)
or [the Recife event](https://rsvp.withgoogle.com/events/search-central-live-recife).

We'll leave the registration open until January 23 and send invitations on Jan 30 to accepted attendees -
you'll need that email to enter the event.

We're looking forward to meeting you in person and chatting about Google Search and Google News.
