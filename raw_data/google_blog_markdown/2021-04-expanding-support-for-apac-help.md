Friday, April 9, 2021

[Earlier in 2020](/search/blog/2020/05/pl-tr-forums), we opened Google Search Central Help
Communities in multiple markets like the [Polish](https://support.google.com/webmasters/community?hl=pl),
[Turkish](https://support.google.com/webmasters/community?hl=tr), and
[Arabic](https://support.google.com/webmasters/community?hl=ar) communities.

As we’ve been continuously working to improve support for website owners in more language markets,
we’re very excited to announce the opening of our [Chinese](https://support.google.com/webmasters/community?hl=zh-Hans),
[Korean](https://support.google.com/webmasters/community?hl=ko),
[Vietnamese](https://support.google.com/webmasters/community?hl=vi),
and [Thai](https://support.google.com/webmasters/community?hl=th)
Search Central Help Communities with support from a global team of community managers that are
dedicated to helping [Product
Experts](https://productexperts.withgoogle.com/what-it-is).

For current Search Central Product Experts, we will honor your badge level in the newly opened APAC
communities. If you previously contributed and would like to start contributing again, you can
start posting in the forum. Also, do reach out to your friends who have contributed in the
past.

We look forward to seeing you there!

Posted by [Aaseesh Marina](https://twitter.com/aaseeshmarina), Product Support Manager
