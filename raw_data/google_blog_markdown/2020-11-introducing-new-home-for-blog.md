Thursday, November 19, 2020

Last week we [changed our name to Google Search Central](/search/blog/2020/11/goodbye-google-webmasters) and published our new central site. As part of this site migration, we're also consolidating the Google Webmaster Blogs into one place on the new site.

Today, we're announcing that [this page within the Google Search Central site](/search/blog) is the new home for our blog. We migrated all of the archived blog posts, [dating back to 2005](/search/blog/2005/08/just-getting-started). They're available in the archive drop down menu.

## No action required for your subscription

You don't need to take any action in order to keep getting updates from us. We will redirect the current set of RSS and email subscribers to the new blog URL.

## Translations are coming soon

We're still working on moving translated blog posts, so it may take some time until you see translated content appearing on the blog. When posts are translated, you'll be able to select your language on this site and get the translated content, compared to the previous blog setup where you needed to know the URL.

If you have any questions or comments, you can find us on [Twitter](https://twitter.com/googlesearchc) and in our [Google Search Central Help Community](https://support.google.com/webmasters/community).
