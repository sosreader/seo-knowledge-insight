# Search Central Live 2024 is coming to Kuala Lumpur and Taipei

Tuesday, Aug 27, 2024

As previously announced, [Search Central Live](/search/events/search-central-live) is coming to **Kuala Lumpur** and to **Taipei** in the last quarter of 2024. And yes, we're very excited!

What can you expect? A whole day of learning about Search while having some fun along the way! Similar to other SCL events, you will get to network with friends and professionals from the same industry and with similar interests - Search and SEO. You will also get to listen to presentations from Googlers flying in specially for these events. There will be plenty of talks thoughtfully picked for each location's audience, including:

* How Search works: the longer version
* About updates and content quality
* AI and Search: 2024 version
* Topics related to ecommerce sites

## Search Central Live Kuala Lumpur 2024

![Search Central Live Kuala Lumpur 2024](/static/search/blog/images/search-central-live-apac/search-central-live-kl-2024-logo.png)

Search Central Live is coming to Kuala Lumpur (instead of Singapore) this year. The event will happen on **October 17**, 2024 (Thursday) and the talks are going to be in **English**. Fill out the registration form for the Kuala Lumpur event before September 29 for a chance to secure a spot. You will receive a **confirmation email by October 6** if we can confirm your seat.

[Register for Search Central Live Kuala Lumpur 2024](https://rsvp.withgoogle.com/events/search-central-live-kl-2024)

## Search Central Live Taipei 2024

![Search Central Live Taipei 2024](/static/search/blog/images/search-central-live-apac/search-central-live-taipei-2024-logo.png)

If you are **fluent in Mandarin**, join us in Taipei. The event is scheduled for **November 1** (Friday). It will be conducted mainly in Mandarin (Traditional Chinese). Interested? Register your interest for the Taipei event before October 14, 2024 and save the date. Since there is limited space, you will receive a **confirmation email by October 21**, 2024 if your spot is confirmed.

[Read in Traditional Chinese and register for Search Central Live Taipei 2024](https://rsvp.withgoogle.com/events/search-central-live-taipei-2024)

If you have read this far, use the registration code "`searchcentral`" when registering! We can’t guarantee the seats, but this will help us in the selection process.
