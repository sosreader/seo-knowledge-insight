Thursday, September 12, 2024

We're excited to announce the launch of our new YouTube series: [Google Trends Tutorials](https://www.youtube.com/playlist?list=PLKoqnv2vTMUO8MUpCbVIiBaDZYrpNF8f-)!
The goal of the series is to help you make the most of [Google Trends](https://trends.google.com/trends/),
a tool that can provide valuable insights into what people are searching for on Google and YouTube.

In each episode we cover a different aspect of Google Trends, from basic usage to advanced techniques.
We'll show you how to find what is trending around you and across the world, and how to dive deep
into the search interest across Google and YouTube searches. These video tutorials will help you use
Google Trends data to inform your content strategy, marketing campaigns, and other use cases.

In the [Trending Now episode](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=O-9G9k6ogdw),
our latest release, you'll learn how to identify current trends across different countries and regions to help
you tap into trends relevant to you. This feature has been [recently upgraded](https://blog.google/products/search/google-trends-trending-now-update/)
to include more emerging trends, fresher data, and more countries and regions for you to analyze.

Whether you're a beginner or an experienced user of Google Trends, we're confident that you'll learn
something new from this series. In some of the episodes we go pretty deep into different methods for
analyzing the available data. For example, watch the [advanced tips video](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Kkd2PkbCdSc)
embedded here to learn advanced search features such as special characters and detailed comparison filters.

The series is just mid-way through, so be sure to subscribe to the [Search Central YouTube channel](https://www.youtube.com/@GoogleSearchCentral?sub_confirmation=1)
to be the first to watch our upcoming episodes. If you have any suggestions about the series, feel free
to leave a comment in the videos or reply to our posts on [X](https://x.com/googlesearchc)
or [Linkedin](https://www.linkedin.com/showcase/googlesearchcentral/about/).

Stay tuned!
