## What to expect

[Search Central Live Deep Dive (SCL DD)](/search/blog/2025/03/search-central-live-deep-dive-apac-2025#introducing-search-central-live-deep-dive)
is a new event format designed based on your feedback at previous SCL events and the interest
form:

* **It's longer**; multiple days versus a few hours.
* **More in-depth**; we have more time to elaborate instead of picking very specific
  topics.
* **Wider range of topics**; again, because we have more time.
* For full comparison, see the
  [Search Central Live Deep Dive page on Search Central](/search/events/search-central-live-deep-dive).

The Search Central Live Deep Dive Asia Pacific 2025 event is suitable for anyone who's interested
in or has a profession related to search, web development, or SEO. We have no formal requirements
for eligible registrations, but we think that a basic understanding of the internet, digital
marketing, and search will be helpful throughout the event.

We spent the past month coming up with an agenda and we already know the larger themes.

**Day one:** After the opening keynote, we start with a high level overview of search
and AI systems, and how they connect to the internet. We then dive deep into crawling related
topics and how to use Search Console to understand how your site is crawled.

**Day two**: Building on the previous day's topics, we explore the different stages
of indexing, as well as how non-textual content is indexed. We will also take a look at more
specific topics such as ecommerce and internationalization, and how to debug indexing related
issues with Search Console.

**Day three:** With the knowledge acquired on day two, we explore how search results
are actually created and ranked, but also how they're presented as different Search features. We
take a critical look at how performance issues can be diagnosed in Search Console, and how to
find more content opportunities using Google Trends.

We will publish a schedule with minute breakdown closer to the event.

## Register now and save the date

The first ever Search Central Deep Dive debuts in
**Bangkok, Thailand on July 23-25, 2025**. As you can see the event is 3 days long
this year, and we're looking for attendees who can commit to all 3 days. If that's you, register
by June 28. The attendance is free of charge as always, however, since the number of seats are
limited, we will
**notify you if your spot is confirmed within a few weeks and, the latest, by July 2, 2025**.

[Register for Search Central Live Deep Dive Asia Pacific 2025](https://rsvp.withgoogle.com/events/search-central-live-dd-apac-2025)

The participant selection is open to anyone who wishes to travel to Bangkok, Thailand, for this
event. We cannot offer help with
[travel logistics, visa, or accommodations](/search/events/search-central-live#expandable-2).
This event will be primarily catered to an audience based in Asia-Pacific, meaning the content,
examples, and style of activities will be focused on those markets.

If you read this far, use the registration code `SearchCentral` when registering.

## Showcase your work

During the registration, remember to pitch ideas for your
[lightning talk](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Lightning_talk) or
[poster session](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Poster_session)
for a chance to showcase your knowhow, experience, and case studies! We are looking for any topic
that you think might be helpful for the attendees, and also more specific things, for example
using AI to understand your site or audience better, optimizing crawling on a large site, or best
URL structure for toddlers; go wild!

**Submit your pitch along with your registration by May 21** and, if shortlisted,
we will get in touch with you about your talk by May 28.

We're incredibly excited about this new event format and can't wait to connect with you.
