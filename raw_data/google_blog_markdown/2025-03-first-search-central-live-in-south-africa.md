Monday, March 3, 2025

On April 2, 2025 we'll be in Johannesburg, South Africa for the very first Search Central Live event in Africa!

![John Mueller on stage at a previous Search Central Live event](/static/search/blog/images/search-central-live-south-africa-2025/johnmu-on-stage.jpg)

We're looking forward to an afternoon with a packed program of presentations, Q&A, and networking. We want to meet local publishers, SEOs, digital marketers, website owners and everyone else who's making websites! Apply for a seat by March 9!

[Learn more & apply for an invitation](https://rsvp.withgoogle.com/events/scl-south-africa-25)

The event will be held from 2pm to 5pm with networking drinks until 6pm afterwards, so apply for a spot and meet John Mueller, Martin Splitt, the local Google team and lots of new people from your community.
We're excited to welcome you at Search Central Live South Africa and talk about all things Google Search!
