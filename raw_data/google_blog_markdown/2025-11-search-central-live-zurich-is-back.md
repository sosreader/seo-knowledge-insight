# Search Central Live is back in Zurich!

Tuesday, November 11, 2025

We're thrilled to announce our upcoming [Search Central Live Zurich](https://rsvp.withgoogle.com/events/scl-zurich-25) on December 9th!
This is a fantastic opportunity for SEO and digital marketing professionals to connect, learn and engage with each other and the Google Search teams.

[Apply for a seat](https://rsvp.withgoogle.com/events/scl-zurich-25)

![Search Central Live Zurich on stage](/static/search/blog/images/search-central-live-emea/search-central-live-zurich-logo-on-stage.jpg)

Get ready for insightful presentations from various Google Search teams, including Search Console,
Search Relations, Shopping and others. You'll gain valuable knowledge directly from the experts, helping you stay ahead in the ever-evolving world of search.

Beyond the presentations, the event offers excellent networking opportunities with fellow professionals from the SEO and digital marketing industries.
Share experiences, exchange ideas, and build valuable connections.
Plus, you'll have the chance to participate in Q&A sessions with Googlers, getting your burning questions answered directly.

![People networking at Search Central Live Zurich](/static/search/blog/images/search-central-live-emea/search-central-live-zurich-networking.jpg)

This year we will also include Lightning Talks from community members again - these 10 minute sessions offer the stage to speakers to share their insights and ideas with the audience.
If you're interested, [fill in this form to apply for a slot!](https://forms.gle/bZ2s4PrPCJuyjxWs6)

[Apply for a seat](https://rsvp.withgoogle.com/events/scl-zurich-25) at the event until November 19th! Confirmations will be sent out on an ongoing basis but you'll hear from us with a definite response by the 24th of November. Don't miss out on this unique chance to enhance your skills and network with your community!
We look forward to seeing you there!
