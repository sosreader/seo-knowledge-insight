Friday, September 12, 2025

Get ready, Japan! We're thrilled to announce the return of Search Central Live Tokyo on Nov 7,
2025! **If you are fluent in Japanese** and are interested, read on! We're bringing
back all the elements you loved, with even more opportunities for you to shape the experience.

![Search Central Live Tokyo 2025](/static/search/blog/images/search-central-live-apac/scl-ja-2025/search-central-live-tok-2025-site-logo.png)

## Lightning Talks: share your expertise!

One of the most popular features of
[Search Central Live Tokyo 2023](/search/blog/2023/07/search-central-live-tok-jkt-2023-recap)
was the lightning talks. We loved the energy and diverse insights shared by the community, and we
can't wait to see what you'll bring to the stage this year! Do you have a burning SEO tip, a
fascinating case study, a new tool discovery, or a unique perspective on search that you can share
in a concise, impactful presentation? We want to hear from you!

**Why submit a lightning talk?**

* **Showcase your expertise**: Share your knowledge with a highly engaged audience of
  fellow SEO professionals.
* **Network**: Connect with others who share your passions and interests.
* **Contribute to the community**: Help shape the learning experience for everyone.

Are you **fluent in Japanese** and ready to propose your talk? You can submit your
lightning talk proposal starting today before October 5! Visit our official event page for details
and the submission form.

## What do you want to hear about?

We'll be collecting your desired topics during the sign-up process: this is your chance to tell us
exactly what you want to learn more about in the world of search. From advanced technical SEO to
content strategies, algorithm updates, or AI, — no topic is too niche!

We'll use your valuable feedback to craft an agenda that's highly relevant and beneficial to all
attendees of different backgrounds. So, when you register, be sure to let us know what's on your
mind!

## Register now!

Ready to learn, network, and contribute? You can already apply as a participant today, and submit
your lightning talk proposal! Secure your chance to attend and tell us what topics you want to see
covered — or what YOU want to talk about, by registering here:

[Read in Japanese and Apply](/search/blog/2025/09/scl-tok?hl=ja)

Note that the capacity is limited. If you are selected, we will send you a confirmation email as
early as possible and by **October 29.** Registration will remain open until
October 20.

## Event Details

**Date**: Nov 7, 2025
**Location**: Google Japan - Shibuya

As always, Search Central Live Tokyo will conclude with happy hour, providing the perfect
opportunity to relax, network, and continue those engaging conversations. And, if space allows,
we might even have a dedicated corner for some classic games like The Tower of Crawling and
Indexing or a friendly round of dinosaur-themed fun!

We can't wait to see you there! Eat all the tsukemen and get ready for a day of insightful
learning, engaging discussions, and valuable networking.
