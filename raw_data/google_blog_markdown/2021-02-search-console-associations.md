# New Association functionality added to Search Console
- **發佈日期**: 2021-02-01
- **作者**: Google Search Central
- **來源 URL**: https://developers.google.com/search/blog/2021/02/search-console-associations
- **來源類型**: article
- **來源集合**: google-search-central
---
Monday, February 22, 2021

We're happy to announce that Search Console has an enhanced [Association page](https://search.google.com/search-console/settings/associations)
within the settings menu. Association is a connection between a Search Console property, and some other entity or property in another Google service: for example, between a Search Console property and a Google Analytics account, a YouTube channel, or a Chrome Web Store account.

Any existing associations are automatically carried over to the new Associations page.
Read more about [how to use associations](https://support.google.com/webmasters/answer/9419894).

![Google Search Console Associations page](/static/search/blog/images/search-console-associations.png "Google Search Console Associations page")

Associating your site with the following Google services can unlock more functionality for your site. Search Console supports the following association types:

* [Google Analytics](https://support.google.com/analytics/answer/1308621) property: View your
  Search Console data, such as organic search queries, in your Google Analytics property. Starting today, you can also link
  Search Console [domain properties](https://support.google.com/webmasters/answer/34592) to Google Analytics
  and get better coverage of your organic search results. We recommend using this option as it can help you get a more complete
  picture in the [Search Console reports](https://support.google.com/analytics/answer/1308626) in Google Analytics.* [Google Ads](https://support.google.com/google-ads/answer/7529561) account: View the paid and
    organic report in Google Ads.* [YouTube](https://support.google.com/youtube/answer/2887282) channel: Link your videos to your associated website.* [Play Console](https://support.google.com/googleplay/android-developer/answer/6041489) app: Use App Indexing.* [Actions Console](/assistant/console/brand-verification) project: Verify your brand and enable cross-platform functionality.* [Chrome Web Store](https://support.google.com/chrome/a/answer/2714278) account: Publish apps and extensions to the Chrome Web Store on behalf of your site.

For questions or comments on the new Associations page, you can drop by our
[Search Central help forums](https://support.google.com/webmasters/go/community),
or contact us through our [other help channels](/search/help).
Also see our [Associations help page](https://support.google.com/webmasters/answer/9419894) to learn
more about how to associate your Search Console property with another Google service.
