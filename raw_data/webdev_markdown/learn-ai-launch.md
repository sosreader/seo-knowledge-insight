Published: February 5, 2026

We know, AI is everywhere. Every company is touting new AI features,
integrations, and more. There's hundreds, if not thousands of models to choose
from, everyone is competing for your attention. How do you make those choices?

Introducing our new course that aims to answer those questions:
[Learn AI](/learn/ai). This course was written by
[Dr. Janna Lipenkova](https://www.linkedin.com/in/janna-lipenkova-07137399/),
author of the [Art of AI Product Development](https://www.google.com/books/edition/The_Art_of_AI_Product_Development/XHZmEQAAQBAJ),
with a welcome message from [Alexandra Klepper](https://alexandrascript.com).

This is `web.dev`, which means our course is written for web developers. We
don't get into the weeds of the mathematical principles and algorithms behind
building your own model. Instead, we offer structures to support your technical
leadership, building a responsible system for successful features.

We recommend you take this course in order, as the introduction lays a
foundation of AI system blueprints: how do you build a system, where do you
build it, and what problems does it solve?

Discover some new best practices and build your expertise in thoughtful AI
deployment.
