Wednesday, May 27, 2020

We published episode 1 of our webtoon series in Korean. Here's a summary of the episode in English:

Cherry Muffin, a diehard fan of a K-Pop band, runs a website for the fans of her favorite band. One day, Cherry realizes her pages are not discovered on Google Search, and her friend Eco Bag introduces *Chisori* to her. *Chisori* is a personified character of Search Console and a word play on *chit-sole* which means toothbrush in Korean (remember, you need to use important tools as often as you brush your teeth). While Cherry waits for her website data to surface in Search Console, *Chisori* explains how Google Search works.
