Monday, February 6, 2023

[Leia em Português](/search/blog/2023/02/search-central-live-brazil?hl=pt)

We're very excited to announce that Search Central Live is coming to Brazil! Following our
[successful events](/search/blog/2022/12/search-central-live-2022) last year, we're continuing
our mission to help you enhance your site's performance in Google Search.

![Search Central Live Brazil logo](/static/search/blog/images/search-central-live-brazil.png)

We're organizing three events in Brazil, starting with São Paulo on March 9, then Brasília on March 14, and
finishing with Belo Horizonte on March 16.

While many of our speakers will be from Google Brazil, we're also excited to invite engineers and executives
from the global Google Search and News teams, like Richard Gingras (VP, Google News), Danny Sullivan
(Search Liaison), and John Mueller (Search Relations team lead). Each event will have a slightly different
agenda, but here are a few highlights:

* SEO and Search Console: what are the best practices to succeed in Google Search?
* Google News in Brazil: how are we partnering with news sites to help them prosper?
* Developing Google Search in Brazil: what are we working on locally?

If the topics above sound interesting to you, whether you're an SEO enthusiast, a website owner, or a
publisher, don't miss your chance to participate. And apart from the presentations, you'll have lots
of time to chat with the Google Search and News teams, and meet new people in real life. The events
will not be recorded or live streamed, that's why we call it live!

Unfortunately we can't accommodate everyone. We'll send invitations on Feb 27 to accepted attendees, you'll
need that email to enter the event. Each of the events has its own website; choose the city that works
for you and register to apply for an invitation (all events will be held in the morning):

* [São Paulo, March 9](https://rsvp.withgoogle.com/events/search-central-live-brasil-sao-2023)* [Brasília, March 14](https://rsvp.withgoogle.com/events/search-central-live-brasilia-2023)* [Belo Horizonte, March 16](https://rsvp.withgoogle.com/events/search-central-live-brasil-bh-2023)

We're looking forward to meeting in person and chatting about Google Search and News.
